Sesame Teriyaki Chicken

Sesame Chicken Teriyaki

Description

This is one of my favorite hommade meals that doesn't take long to cook.

With just a few simple ingredients you, too,can be making this delvious meal!

Ingedients

  • Diced Chicken Breast
  • Green Onions
  • Steamed Rice
  • Sesame Seeds
  • 3 tbsp Sesame Oil
  • 1/4 cup Brown Sugar
  • 1/2 cup Soy Sauce
  • 2 tbsp Mirin (sweet rice wine)
  • 1/4 cup water

Cooking Steps

  1. Saute the chicken breast in sesame oil
  2. In a seperate sauce pan, combine soy sauce, mirin and water and heat to a simmer
  3. Once your sauce is at a simmer, combine the brown sugar and stir until thick
  4. Combine sauce with chicken and sautee for 1 more minute
  5. Sprinkle with sesame seeds, chopped green onion and eat with steamed rice!
  6. Enjoy!
