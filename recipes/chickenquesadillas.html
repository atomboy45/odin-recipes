Chicken Quesadillas

Description

This is one of my other favorite meals that I usually get from taco bell. I'm not gonna lie, when I make it at hommade

it's pretty simple and theres not really much to it. It's just plain chicken and cheese, but still tastes great!

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Fajita Chicken
  • Sliced Jalapeños (I don't use these but feel free to

Steps

  1. Pan crisp your chicken and season with salt, pepper, and anything you'd like

    *don't forget to include yoiur sliced Jalapeños during this if you want to*

  2. Heat up the tortilla on a fry pan or comal until crisp
  3. Once the tortialla is crispy, add some shredded cheese to your liking
  4. Once cheese is melty, add the chicken and clip the tortilla in half over
  5. Enjoy!
